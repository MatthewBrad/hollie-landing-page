About Me

My name is Hollie Bradley and I obtained my Masters in Programme Evaluation at the University of Cape Town in 2015 and have been working in the field ever since.

I am a monitoring and evaluation (M&E) consultant offering a wide variety of services to cover all your data needs, whether that is an M&E system from scratch, M&E focused workshops or evaluations.

My focus is on sharing my passion for M&E to assist NPOs in making M&E work for them, their donors and their beneficiaries.

My Values

As a practitioner whose passion lies in the NPO space, I truly value:

  • Capacity building – M&E is often seen as a burden or a chore, with my passion I intend to aid staff to see the benefits of M&E and how they can make it work for them
  • Collaboration – any sustainable M&E system must be built in collaboration with the organisations staff, those with the most experience and expertise with regards to the programmes.
  • Simplification – M&E solutions made simple is my aim, a user-friendly system that works for the programme, staff, beneficiaries, and donors.
